News August 2018

2018/05/10

Since our last newsletter in May a few things have happened: - In June we had our 4th and last meetup before the summer break - R-Ladies launched a new Community Slack - and we caught up with two of our speakers in an interview.

We are currently planning our meetups in Autumn, with the first meetup being in October.

Check our meetup page for updates.

We are also looking for speakers and venues, please contact us via lausanne@rladies.org if you are interested.
